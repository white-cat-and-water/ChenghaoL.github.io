Research Interest

 I work in the field of Intelligent sensing systems, Time series, Reinforcement learning, Spatio-temporal data mining, Anomaly detection, Audio, Generative model, Machine learning, and Deep learning. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Spatio-temporal data mining and Distributed signal processing
  • Time series based anomaly detection and Physical-based machine learning
  • Audio and Generative model
