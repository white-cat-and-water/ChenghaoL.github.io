Research Interest

 I work in the field of Sensors Network, Data Mining, Federated Learning, AI-IoT, Graph Neural Network and Machine Learning. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Temporal data mining and Distributed signal processing
  • Time series based anomaly detection and Physical-based machine learning
  • Incremental label distribution learning
  • Federated Incremental learning
